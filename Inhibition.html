Inhibition Task

Two animal cards appear side by side.


Press SPACE only when both cards show the same animal and both are right-side up.


Each trial lasts 1.5 s. The session lasts 60 s.


Beware of pairs showing the same animal with one card sneakily flipped upside down — they are almost right... but only almost. Mwahahaha.

Time's Up!
